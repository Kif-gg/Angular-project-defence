How long is the waiting until you view my request for repair services?

Our workers, from the mechanics to the administration team, all work Monday-Saturday from 08:00 to 17:00. Sunday is a non-work day. So expect your request approved in the given period of time if it meets the requirements.

My account is blocked, what should I do?

Usually, the account gets blocked if you enter wrong login data several times. This is done for security measures to prevent hacking. If you account is blocked or you have suspicion that you might have been hacked, contact us here.

Do you deliver cars to demanded places?

We have drivers and platforms that can transport your vehicle wherever you want in the country, this service is available in all provinces.

How long will it take if I chose delivery option from you?

It depends on where you want your delivery, and how far it is the place of the person you are buying from, also what time of the day you order the delivery. If the distance is more than 300 km and you order for example at 15:00 o'clock, we will send our drivers on the day tomorrow.

If our administration team sees that the request does not provide enough info, or that it is suspicious of scam, they do not approve it. We recommend that you check your email for more information about why your request wasn't approved.
